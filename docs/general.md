### Terra Invicta 1.0 Data Block: General Context

**1. Meta Information**

* **Version:** 1.0 (Full Release)
* **Release Date:** 5 January 2026
* **Platform/Engine:** Windows / Unity
* **Developer:** Pavonis Interactive

**2. Core Premise**

* **Genre**: Grand Strategy / 4X / Geopolitical Simulator.
* **Setting**: Near-future Earth (starting 2022, 2026, or 2070) facing an imminent alien arrival.
* **Perspective**: Players lead one of seven ideological factions rather than a nation. Control over Earth is exercised through shadow influence, espionage, and political subversion.

**3. Core Pillars**

* **Geopolitical Layer:** Factions compete for "Control Points" (CP) within Earth nations to steer GDP, military, and research.
* **Councilor Layer:** Turn-based RPG mechanics using agents for espionage, sabotage, and public relations.
* **Industrial Layer:** Expansion from Earth to the Solar System via mining outposts (Habs) and orbital stations.
* **Tactical Layer:** Newtonian 3D space combat involving modular ship design and heat management.

**4. Faction Directives**
| Faction | Primary Objective | Key Win Condition (spoiler warning) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Resistance** | Defence | Seal the wormhole; maintain human autonomy. |
| **Humanity First** | Extermination | Total destruction of alien assets and collaborators. |
| **The Servants** | Submission | Assist alien takeover; achieve religious dominance. |
| **The Academy** | Cooperation | Negotiate an equal partnership with the aliens. |
| **The Initiative** | Profit | Control the flow of space resources for corporate gain. |
| **Project Exodus** | Escape | Launch a generation ship to an interstellar system. |
| **The Protectorate** | Appeasement | Negotiate a non-aggression pact through surrender. |

**5. Strategic Resource Types**

* **Water/Volatiles:** Life support and propellant.
* **Metals/Noble Metals:** Hull construction and electronics.
* **Fissiles:** Nuclear drives and reactors.
* **Exotics:** Alien-derived material for end-game tech.

**6. Victory Stages**

* **Stage 1:** Consolidate Earth power (Control Points).
* **Stage 2:** Establish Lunar/Martian mining (Boost vs. In-situ resources).
* **Stage 3:** Technological parity (The "Tech Tree" rush).
* **Stage 4:** Interplanetary war and Faction-specific mission completion.
